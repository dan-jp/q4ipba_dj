Halo-Halo

Easy Direct Understanding

“Hello kiddos! Does init hits you? Because its SUMMER time!” Today the team EDU would like to present to you our first educational video related to a traditional dessert of the Philippines! Halo-halo, a Filipino dessert with pre-colonial roots, has been influenced by Japanese and Spanish culinary traditions. It evolved from Japanese kakigori and Spanish granizado, with Filipinos incorporating local ingredients to reflect their rich cultural heritage.
